Beef and Broccoli

Discription

This recipe shows you how to make a quick, easy, and delicious beef and broccoli. This will give you a resturant style meal from the confort of your home. This 20 minute recipe will become a staple of your household if you follow these steps and cook it with care!

Ingredients for the Beef and Broccoli

  • Sizzle steak
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb broccoli
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds, optional garnish

Ingredients for the Stir Fry Sauce

  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp garlic, grated(from 3 cloves)
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 6 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 Tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp corn starch
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Put olive oil in a pan and let it get hot
  2. Cook a boiler of rice for 15 minutes
  3. Season and cook your sizzle steak in the pan
  4. Cook your broccoli in the pan with olive oil and add water and cover to let it steam
  5. Add together all of your ingredients for the stir fry sauce
  6. Mix in your beef and broccoli then add the stirfry sauce and let cook for 3 - 5 minutes
  7. Assemble your beef and broccoli with rice
  8. TIME TO EAT!
