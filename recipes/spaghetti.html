Spaghetti

Discription

This recipe shows you how to make a quick, easy, and delicious spaghetti. This will give you an italian style meal from the confort of your home. This 20 minute recipe will be great to eat and last you a day or two!

Ingredients for the Spaghetti

  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • 2 pounds of Ground chuck
  • 2 24 ounce jars of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Seasoning salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook your ground chuck in a pan
  2. Strain the ground chuck
  3. Cook your onion, bell pepper, and garlic in a pan
  4. Mix ground chuck, onion, bell pepper, and garlic in a pan. Season and cook for 3 minutes
  5. Add spaghetti noodles to boiling water and cook for 12 minutes
  6. Add sauce to a pot and season it to taste as it warms up
  7. Add ground chuck, onion, bell pepper, and garlic mix to your sauce. Season to taste again
  8. Add spaghetti noodles to your sauce
  9. Assemble your spaghetti and top it off with parmesan cheese and italian seasoning
  10. TIME TO EAT!
