Rotel

Discription

This recipe shows you how to make a quick, easy, and delicious Rotel. This will give you a gameday feel with every bite. Follow our directions to make a cheesy, creamy, delicious rotel that rivals all others.

Ingredients for the Rotel

  • Velveeta cheese
  • Philadelphia cream cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Ground sausage
  • Doritos or Tortilla chips
  • 2 cans of rotel
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper

Steps

  1. Slice your velveeta cheese and philadelphia cream cheese and add it to your crock pot. Stir every 5 minutes
  2. Cook your ground beef and sausage
  3. Strain your ground beef and sausage
  4. Cook your onions and bell peppers.
  5. Add together ground beef, sausage, onions, and bell peppers and season to taste
  6. Mix in your ground beef, sausage, onions, and bell peppers to your crockpot and stir everything together. Season to taste
  7. Add your chips to a plate and pour your rotel over the top
  8. TIME TO EAT!
